Hiring Process

We source a large candidate pool, apply multiple screening filters, and present you with 2–4 top finalists. You conduct the final interviews and make the hiring decision.

We combine personality testing (Culture Index), intelligence testing (UCAT), resume and experience reviews, and cultural fit interviews to ensure we find the right people for your organization.

Typically, the hiring process takes 4–6 weeks from kickoff to your team member's start date, depending on the complexity of the role and your interview availability.
